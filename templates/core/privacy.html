Privacy Policy

Welcome to A.L.F.A Errortronics, the tech treasure trove tucked away in someone's grandma's basement. Our Privacy Policy is as quirky as our location, so let's dive right in:

1. Data Collection

We collect data as if it's the forgotten treasures at the back of Grandma's closet. We may gather your name, email, and address, but rest assured, your secrets are safe with us. Grandma's locked the closet.

2. Cookies

We do use cookies, but not the digital kind. Ours are made from Grandma's secret recipe and may disappear faster than you'd like. Unfortunately, we can't provide the recipe; it's a closely guarded family secret.

3. Data Security

Your data is as safe as Grandma's famous meatloaf recipe. We protect it with all our might, although Grandma's might is considerable, especially when defending the meatloaf from Buttons, the mischievous cat.

4. Cat Interference

If you hear a meow during your visit, don't be alarmed. It's just Buttons, our furry assistant, who occasionally walks on the keyboard and sends you gibberish emails. We apologize in advance.

5. Third Parties

We won't share your data with third parties unless Grandma's cookies qualify as a third-party treat. If they do, rest assured, they're delicious and won't misuse your information.

6. Grandma's Wisdom

Our customer support is powered by Grandma's age-old wisdom. You might receive advice on both life and technology. She believes that a good laugh can fix almost anything, and she's often right.

7. Changes to the Policy

If we ever update our Privacy Policy, you'll be the first to know. Grandma might send you a handwritten letter with a new cookie recipe. Keep an eye on your mailbox!

8. Legal Stuff

While we're a bit unconventional, we take your privacy seriously. If you have any concerns or questions, please reach out. Grandma will respond personally, and you might even get a story from her younger days.

By visiting A.L.F.A Errortronics, you agree to this whimsical Privacy Policy. Thanks for being a part of our basement-based adventure!
